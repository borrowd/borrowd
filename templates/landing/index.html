Welcome to Borrow'd!

Borrow’d is a community-powered platform that helps you share with the people you trust in real life. It’s built around Groups - your friends, neighbors, and community members.

If you’re not in any Groups, you won’t see any items available to borrow - because sharing only happens within your chosen communities.

To join a group, use the invitation link that you received.

No invitation link? Create your own group, add some items to share, and invite your friends to join!
